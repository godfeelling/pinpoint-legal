Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the PinPoint – World Quiz, Flags & Capitals app ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Data Collected

The App does not collect personally identifiable information directly. We do not ask for your name, email, address, or any personal data.

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Language and sound preferences.
  • Game statistics (scores, achievements).

This data is never transmitted to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers.
  • IP address (for approximate geolocation).
  • Ad interaction data.

For more information, see the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us so we can delete it.

5. Security

Game data is stored only locally on your device. We do not transmit or store data on external servers.

6. Your Rights

You can delete all local App data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its data in your device settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the revised date.
